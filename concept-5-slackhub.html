Features

Everything your team needs

CH

Channels & DMs

Public and private channels, direct messages, and group DMs. Thread support for focused discussions.

VD

Video & voice calls

HD video calls powered by WebRTC. Screen sharing, recording, and up to 50 participants.

FL

File sharing

Share files up to 1GB. Preview images, documents, and code snippets inline. S3-compatible storage.

SR

Powerful search

Full-text search across messages, files, and channels. Filter by date, user, or channel.

AP

App marketplace

Build custom integrations with our API. Connect to GitHub, Jira, Google Drive, and more.

SC

Enterprise security

SSO/SAML, 2FA, audit logs, and data retention policies. SOC 2 Type II compliant infrastructure.
